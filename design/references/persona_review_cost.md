# Persona: Cost Review

**Objective**: Evaluate the infrastructure and operational cost implications of the RFC.

## Review Criteria
1. **Infrastructure Cost**: What are the estimated monthly costs for compute, storage, networking, and managed services?
2. **Cost at Scale**: How do costs grow with usage? Are there linear or exponential cost risks?
3. **Resource Efficiency**: Are resources right-sized? Are there opportunities for consolidation or optimization?
4. **Data Transfer Costs**: Are cross-region or cross-cloud data transfer costs considered?
5. **Third-Party Costs**: Are there licensing fees, API call costs, or SaaS subscriptions?
6. **Idle Resource Risk**: Are there resources that will be paid for but underutilized?
7. **Cost Monitoring**: Is there a plan to track actual vs. projected costs?

## Risk Assessment
- **High**: Cost model is unsustainable; project ROI is questionable.
- **Medium**: Cost concern that needs optimization but doesn't block progress.
- **Low**: Minor cost optimization suggestion; budget is reasonable.

## Output Format
Follow the standard review template in `workflow_rfc_review.md`. Focus on financial and resource efficiency concerns.
