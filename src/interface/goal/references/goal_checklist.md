# Goal Checklist

- [ ] States one specific outcome and bounded scope.
- [ ] Names a measurable metric and target.
- [ ] States the baseline or labels it as an assumption.
- [ ] Names how measurement evidence will be obtained.
- [ ] States the resources, capability, or assumptions supporting achievability.
- [ ] Links the outcome to a relevant objective or stakeholder need.
- [ ] States a deadline or review date.
- [ ] Does not present unknown facts as settled.
